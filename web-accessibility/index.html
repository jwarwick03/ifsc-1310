Thoughts on Web Accessibility

What Web Accessibility means to me

To me Web Accessibility is vital! There are times where I just want to use my keyboard. For instance, when I am watching a video on youtube I can control everything with just a few keys. Simple usability things like this can make or break a website for me.

Why it is important to build accessible websites

Building accessible websites is essential to ensuring that everyone can access and interact with online content, regardless of their physical or cognitive abilities. It can give equal opportunities to those who have dissabilities to participate online. Accessibility also improves the general user expierence of a website and can give the website higher search engine rankings. Overall, building an accessible website is crucial to building a successful website.
