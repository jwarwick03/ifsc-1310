Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

The progressive enhancement perspective shows the importance of content. Content is described as the foundation of the three layers; HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These three layers can be seen as a peanut M&M, the peanut is the content (HTML), the chocolate coating is the presentation (CSS), and the candy coating is the client- side scripting (JavaScript). Progressive enhancement is important to the modern web because it is the blueprint for building a website.
